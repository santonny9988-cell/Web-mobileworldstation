About the AirPods Max

The AirPods Max are Apple’s premium wireless over-ear headphones with features like:

  • Pro-level Active Noise Cancellation to block out unwanted sound.

  • Transparency Mode so you can still hear your surroundings.

  • Personalised Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking for immersive sound.

  • Up to ~20 hours of battery life per charge.

  • Adaptive EQ, automatic device switching with Apple devices, and easy setup.

Recent versions have USB-C instead of Lightning, aligning with newer Apple devices.
