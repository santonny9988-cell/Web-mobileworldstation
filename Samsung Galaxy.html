Samsung Galaxy Z Fold7 – Highlights

  • Foldable flagship with a tablet-like large screen + phone-like cover display.

  • Ultra-thin and light design – about 215 g and 8.9 mm folded / 4.2 mm unfolded.

  • Dual displays:
    • 8.0″ inner Dynamic AMOLED 2X (high refresh, great for multitasking).
    • 6.5″ cover screen with 21:9 ratio for easier one-hand use.

  • Performance: Snapdragon 8 Elite for Galaxy chipset + up to 16 GB RAM.

  • Cameras:
    • 200 MP main lens (first in the series).
    • Additional telephoto and ultrawide sensors for versatile shooting.

  • Software: One UI 8 with Galaxy AI features + Android 16.

  • Battery & charging: ~4400 mAh battery with ~25 W wired charging.

  • Durability: IP48 water/dust resistance.

  • Storage: Options include 256 GB, 512 GB, and 1 TB.
