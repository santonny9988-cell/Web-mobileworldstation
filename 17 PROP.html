What the iPhone 17 Air is

  • A sleek, ultra-slim member of Apple’s iPhone 17 series, designed to be the thinnest iPhone ever at around 5.6 mm thickness with a lightweight build.

  • It slots between the standard and Pro models in price and features, offering a premium feel without the full high-end Pro specs.

💡 Key Features

  • Display: ~6.5 – 6.6-inch high-refresh OLED (ProMotion up to 120 Hz).

  • Chip: Apple A19 Pro series silicon for strong performance.

  • Design: Titanium frame, ceramic shield for durability, very thin body.

  • Camera: Advanced rear camera system with high-megapixel sensor(s).

  • Network & features: 5G, Wi-Fi 7, USB-C, eSIM-only on most units.
